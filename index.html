Funding schemes for latinoamerican students and researchers / Becas para Latinoamérica

A list of funding schemes and opportunities for latinoamerican students and researchers at all levels (Undergraduate, Master, PhD & Postdoc), created by Pablo J. Sáez @pj_saez & @ECR_LARC & eLife Ambassadors latam Tweet

We encourage the Latinoamerican (Latam) scientific community to help to add new funding opportunities to the list by using the submit funding form in the tab down here. Be aware that the information you will provide fulfilling the form is moderated by @ECR_LARC so it will not appear automatically here.
